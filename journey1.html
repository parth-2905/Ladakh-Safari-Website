Day 01

Arrive Leh

Welcome arrival at leh airport and transfer to hotel. Complete Day for rest and leisure to acclimatize. Evening visit leh market, Leh palace & shanti stupa. Overnight at hotel in Leh.

Day 02

Leh ‚Äì Sangam

Post breakfast we drive on the Srinagar highway and start our day by visiting the Hall of Fame- Museum constructed by the Indian army, Gurudwara Patthar Sahib nestled deep in the Himalayas, which was built by the lamas of Leh in 1517 to commemorate the visit of Guru Nanak Dev. Further we experience the Magnetic Hill which defies the law of gravity. We stop at the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar Rivers and later we drive back to the hotel in Leh.

Day 03

Excursion to Khardung La Top

Post breakfast we drive to Khardung La - the highest motorable road in the world at 18,380 ft. The top is 39 kms from Leh. Enjoy the views from the top and then return to the hotel. Afternoon free for leisure and evening visit Leh market. Overnight at hotel in Leh.
