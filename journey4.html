JOURNEY 2: LADAKH PARADISE

Journey Overview

Travel the Silk Route in Ladakh, exploring ancient monasteries, dramatic landscapes, and vibrant culture. Visit Leh, Sham Valley, Nubra Valley, and Pangong Lake.

Duration: 04 nights / 05 Days
Season: All Year
Destinations: Leh, Sham Valley, Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake

Day 01

Arrive Leh

Welcome arrival at Leh Airport and transfer to the hotel.
A complete day for rest and leisure to acclimatize.
In the evening, visit Leh Market, Leh Palace, and Shanti Stupa.
Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 02

Leh ‚Äì Sham Valley Tour

Post breakfast, we drive on the Srinagar Highway and start our day by visiting the Hall of Fame ‚Äì a museum constructed by the Indian Army.
Next, visit Gurudwara Patthar Sahib, nestled deep in the Himalayas, built by the lamas of Leh in 1517 to commemorate the visit of Guru Nanak Dev.
Further, experience the Magnetic Hill, which defies the law of gravity.
We then stop at the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar Rivers, and later drive back to the hotel in Leh.
Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 03

Leh to Nubra Valley

After an early breakfast, we drive to Nubra Valley, crossing Khardung-La Pass ‚Äì the highest motorable road in the world at 18,380 ft.
Nubra Valley is situated to the north of Ladakh, between the Karakoram and Ladakh ranges of the Himalayas.
Visit the Diskit Monastery and the sand dunes, where you may enjoy a camel safari (optional).
Check in at the resort and enjoy your evening at the campsite.
Overnight stay in Nubra.

Day 04

Nubra Valley to Pangong via Shayok (New Route)

After breakfast at the camp, we drive to Pangong Lake via Shayok ‚Äì a recently opened scenic route.
Pangong Lake, situated at 14,000 ft (4,267 m), is a long, narrow basin of inland drainage, over 130 km in length and 6 to 7 km at its widest point.
It is bisected by the international border between India and China.
Enjoy the breathtaking landscape with the lake as a backdrop. One rarely feels so close to nature ‚Äì the scenery is truly unforgettable.
Evening at leisure and overnight stay at the camp near the lake.

Day 05

Pangong Lake to Leh

145 km / 5 hrs

Morning at leisure to explore the beauty of Pangong Lake.
Later, we drive back to Leh via Changla Pass.
Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 06

Depart Leh

Tour concludes.
