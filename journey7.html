Day 01

Day 01: Arrive Leh

In the evening, visit Leh Market and Shanti Stupa.

Day 02

Day 02: Leh ‚Äì Sham Valley Tour

Post breakfast, we drive on the Srinagar Highway and start our day by visiting the Hall of Fame ‚Äì a museum constructed by the Indian Army.

After an early breakfast, we drive to Nubra Valley, crossing Khardung-La Pass ‚Äì the highest motorable road in the world at 18,380 ft.

Overnight stay in Nubra.

Day 04

Day 04: Nubra ‚Äì Same Day Excursion to Turtuk

Post breakfast, we start our journey to the last Indian village on the India‚ÄìPakistan border ‚Äì Turtuk.
En route, visit Diskit Monastery and Hunder Sand Dunes.
Turtuk was captured by the Indian Army during the 1971 war with Pakistan.

Arrive in Turtuk by afternoon. Further, drive to visit Thang, the last village.
After spending some time there, we drive back to the camp in Nubra.

The lake is bisected by the international border between India and China.
